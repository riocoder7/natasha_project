Common Childhood Diseases üè•

ü§ß Common Cold

Symptoms:

  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Coughing
  • Sneezing
  • Fever (sometimes)
  • Irritability

Prevention:

  • Regular hand washing
  • Keep baby away from sick people
  • Clean and disinfect toys
  • Proper ventilation

ü¶† Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV)

Symptoms:

  • Wheezing
  • Rapid breathing
  • Difficulty feeding
  • Lethargy
  • Fever

Prevention:

  • Limit contact with sick people
  • Frequent hand washing
  • Clean surfaces regularly
  • Breastfeeding when possible

üò¢ Colic

Symptoms:

  • Intense crying
  • Fussiness
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Clenched fists
  • Arched back

Prevention:

  • Proper feeding techniques
  • Burping after feeds
  • Gentle rocking
  • White noise therapy

üü° Jaundice

Symptoms:

  • Yellow skin and eyes
  • Poor feeding
  • Lethargy
  • Dark urine
  • High-pitched crying

Prevention:

  • Regular feeding
  • Proper breastfeeding
  • Regular check-ups
  • Monitor bilirubin levels

üë∂ Diaper Rash

Symptoms:

  • Red, inflamed skin
  • Bumps or blisters
  • Scaling or peeling
  • Pain or discomfort
  • Fussiness

Prevention:

  • Frequent diaper changes
  • Gentle cleaning
  • Use barrier creams
  • Allow air exposure

üë∂ Cradle Cap

Symptoms:

  • Greasy, scaly patches
  • Yellow or brown scales
  • Redness
  • Hair loss in affected areas
  • Mild itching

Prevention:

  • Regular gentle washing
  • Use mild baby shampoo
  • Brush with soft brush
  • Keep scalp clean

üëã Hand, Foot, and Mouth Disease

Symptoms:

  • Fever
  • Sore throat
  • Painful blisters in mouth
  • Rash on hands and feet
  • Loss of appetite

Prevention:

  • Good hygiene practices
  • Regular hand washing
  • Clean toys and surfaces
  • Avoid close contact with infected children

üëÇ Ear Infections

Symptoms:

  • Ear pain
  • Fever
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Pulling at ears
  • Fluid drainage

Prevention:

  • Keep ears dry
  • Proper bottle feeding position
  • Regular check-ups
  • Treat colds promptly

üåπ Roseola

Symptoms:

  • High fever

  • Irritability
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Loss of appetite

Prevention:

  • Good hygiene
  • Avoid contact with sick children
  • Regular hand washing
  • Clean toys and surfaces

ü´Å Croup

Symptoms:

  • Barking cough
  • Hoarse voice
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Fever
  • Stridor (high-pitched sound)

Prevention:

  • Regular hand washing
  • Avoid sick contacts
  • Keep child hydrated
  • Humidify air

üî¥ Fifth Disease

Symptoms:

  • Red rash on cheeks
  • Lace-like rash on body
  • Low-grade fever
  • Joint pain
  • Headache

Prevention:

  • Good hand hygiene
  • Avoid sharing utensils
  • Cover mouth when coughing
  • Regular check-ups

ü¶† Impetigo

Symptoms:

  • Red sores
  • Honey-colored crusts
  • Itching
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Fever (sometimes)

Prevention:

  • Keep skin clean
  • Treat cuts and scrapes
  • Don't share towels
  • Regular hand washing

üî¥ Chickenpox

Symptoms:

  • Itchy rash
  • Flu-like symptoms
  • Blisters that scab over
  • Fever
  • Loss of appetite

Prevention:

  • Vaccination
  • Avoid contact with infected children
  • Good hygiene practices
  • Regular hand washing

ü¶† Strep Throat

Symptoms:

  • Severe sore throat
  • Fever
  • Swollen tonsils
  • White patches in throat
  • Difficulty swallowing

Prevention:

  • Good hand hygiene
  • Avoid sharing utensils
  • Cover mouth when coughing
  • Regular check-ups

ü´Å Asthma

Symptoms:

  • Wheezing
  • Coughing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest tightness
  • Difficulty sleeping

Prevention:

  • Use prescribed medications
  • Regular check-ups
  • Keep environment clean

üß† ADHD

Symptoms:

  • Inattention
  • Hyperactivity
  • Impulsivity
  • Difficulty focusing
  • Organization problems

  • Behavioral therapy
  • Regular exercise
  • Structured routine
  • Parent education

ü•ú Food Allergies

Symptoms:

  • Hives or rash
  • Swelling
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Vomiting
  • Anaphylaxis (severe)

  • Carry epinephrine
  • Inform caregivers
  • Avoid cross-contamination

üò¥ Sleep Disorders

Symptoms:

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Night terrors
  • Sleepwalking
  • Snoring
  • Daytime sleepiness

  • Limit screen time
  • Create sleep-friendly environment
  • Regular exercise

ü§í Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms:

  • High fever
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue
  • Cough
  • Sore throat

Prevention:

  • Annual flu vaccination
  • Good hand hygiene
  • Cover mouth when coughing
  • Stay home when sick

üëÅÔ∏è Pink Eye (Conjunctivitis)

Symptoms:

  • Red, itchy eyes
  • Eye discharge
  • Swollen eyelids
  • Light sensitivity
  • Blurred vision

Prevention:

  • Don't touch eyes
  • Don't share towels
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Clean contact lenses properly

üò¥ Mononucleosis (Mono)

Symptoms:

  • Extreme fatigue
  • Sore throat
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Fever
  • Enlarged spleen

Prevention:

  • Avoid sharing drinks and utensils
  • Don't share toothbrushes
  • Practice good hygiene
  • Get adequate rest

üë§ Acne

Symptoms:

  • Pimples and blackheads
  • Whiteheads
  • Red, inflamed skin
  • Oily skin
  • Scarring (in severe cases)

Prevention:

  • Regular face washing
  • Keep hands away from face
  • Use non-comedogenic products
  • Maintain healthy diet

üèÉ Sports Injuries

Common Types:

  • Sprains and strains
  • Fractures
  • Concussions
  • Overuse injuries
  • Growth plate injuries

  • Use appropriate protective gear
  • Follow safety guidelines
  • Take regular breaks
