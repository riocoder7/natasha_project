General Information

Our clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.

We provide care for children from birth through adolescence (0-18 years). This includes:

  • Newborn care
  • Infant check-ups
  • Toddler health
  • School-age children
  • Teenage healthcare services

Appointments

While we recommend scheduling appointments, we do accept walk-in patients for urgent cases. However, priority is given to patients with scheduled appointments.

You can schedule an appointment through:

  • Our online booking system
  • Calling our clinic at 9876543210
  • Visiting our clinic in person

Vaccinations

You can schedule vaccinations through our online booking system or by calling our clinic. We'll help you plan your child's vaccination schedule according to the recommended timeline.

We offer all recommended childhood vaccines including:

  • BCG
  • DPT
  • MMR
  • Polio
  • Hepatitis A & B
  • And more

Check our vaccination page for the complete schedule.

Insurance & Payments

We accept most major insurance plans and Mediclaim policies. Please contact our office for specific insurance-related queries.

We accept:

  • Cash
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • UPI Payments
  • Net Banking

First Visit

Please bring:

  • Previous medical records
  • Vaccination history
  • Insurance card
  • Government ID
  • List of current medications

A typical visit usually takes:

  • Regular check-up: 20-30 minutes
  • First visit: 45-60 minutes
  • Vaccination: 15-20 minutes
